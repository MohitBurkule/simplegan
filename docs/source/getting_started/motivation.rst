Motivation
==========

The field of Machine Learning, especially Deep Learning has took off since the past few years and more people have started getting involved, learning new concepts and implementing them. This framework was built to facilitate the learning process of these people who are new to these concepts and at the same time be a useful tool to experts in deep learning who are looking to experiment with different generateive models. We provide high level APIs that allows users to train a GAN or an Autoencoder with just 3 lines of code.
